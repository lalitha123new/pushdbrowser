Registration-Evaluation process

For registering you into the PUSH-D program, you are requested you to complete an online initial evaluation process which requires you to fill in demographic details and answer a few questions about your mental health and wellbeing.

Your data will be stored in a secure server and used to generate personalized recommendations for you. It would not be shared with any third parties. Any use/ dissemination of the data for research purpose at any point of time will NOT involve any identifying information about any user and will be presented in only in an annonymized, aggregate/summary format.

Why is personal information being requested?

It is important for us and the users to understand the extent to which PUSH-D may be a suitable tool for a given user and whether the user needs to supplement it with face to face professional help.
Based on the responses you provide during to the initial evaluation, you will receive a feedback on the level of depression, anxiety and your current functioning.

You will also receive our recommendation regarding the suitability of PUSH-D app for your current concerns.
