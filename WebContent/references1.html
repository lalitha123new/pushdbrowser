References and External links

Here are a few references and external links relevant to the themes covered in this program. It is not essential to go over these in order to complete or benefit from the Push-D program.

All of these references are available in summary /full text format through simple internet search.

Those of you, who are inclined to know about the scientific basis of some of the aspects/concepts that we have discussed may wish to go over this list.

Many of these are written in scientific journals and are meant for professionals/scientists in the field. But some of the references/links mentioned are meant for laypersons and are hence easy to read through. These are marked in boldface for your convenience.
